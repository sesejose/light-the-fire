LIGHT THE FIRE

It is getting dark and Benjamin needs to collect some branches and pine cones to light a fire. It should be when there is still day light and before the night fall, otherwise it will be difficult to find the right branches. Help him to pick up those branches and light the fire. Click on the dry ones, but be careful and avoid those that are still green - these are not good for fire and you will lose a life.
